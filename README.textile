h1. In Your Face City Trains

h2. Instructions

The goal of the game is to survive as long as you can.
Jump over trains with closed doors and try (as much as possible) to run through trains with open doors to avoid birds and tunnels. Some open trains will give you coffee. When your coffee-meter is full, you have one extra life.

h2. Controls

* Space: Jump or "a" button on Joystick
* R: Restart 
* 0-4 or F0-F4: Set zoom , 0 is fullscreen
* P: Pause game, "start" button on gamepad will pause
* M: Mute sound
* Escape: back to main menu or exit from game if it is in main menu, also "back" in gamepad will works
